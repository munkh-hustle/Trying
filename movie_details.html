Insidious

16+ 8.5/10 2010 1h 43min Horror, Thriller

Insidious is a supernatural horror film that follows the story of a family whose son inexplicably enters a comatose state and becomes a vessel for ghosts in an astral dimension who want to inhabit his body. The parents must delve into the supernatural realm to rescue their son before it's too late. The film is known for its chilling atmosphere, clever scares, and a unique take on the haunted house genre.
